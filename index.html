Research

“Evolution of Meenachil River Protection Council as A Community-Based Environmental Initiative”
The study attempts to qualitatively explore and understand a community-based environmental initiative. The Meenachil River Protection Council is working to find solutions to environmental problems, and the researcher conducted this study to understand the evolution, history, social action, initiatives to protect the environment, and community participation. The study is qualitative, utilizing a semi-structured interview guide and an observation guide 10 to gather information from the respondents. The findings show that the Meenachil River Protection Council has become a model for community-based environmental protection initiatives. The council evolved from a social movement into a community organization. The Meenachil River Protection Council ensures the participation of the local community members to create an impact on environmental protection. The MRPC is led by a diverse group of people with in-depth knowledge in various professions, including environmentalists, climate scientists, advocates, professors, social workers, and teachers. The council has initiated different interventions to develop awareness among community members about the river and its organisms’; connection to human living conditions, whether cultural, social, or environmental. Policy-level changes have been crucial in the context of river protection; the government can easily influence people’s behavior through rules and regulations, which help reduce exploitation of the river. There is a wide range of opportunities for replicating the MRPC’s strategies in other environmentally sensitive areas across Kerala."
  • Explored community participation in environmental protection.
  • Analyzed the MRPC's strategies and impact on the environment.
  • Developed policy recommendations for replicating MRPC’s success.
